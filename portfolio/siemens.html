Summer at Siemens

For the past summer, I worked as a UX design intern at Siemens Corporate Technology. During the 12 weeks, I worked closely with the designers of UXD department , to make complex feel simple and make experience better. I have accomplished 3 projects:

  • ①
  • In the Simit Software Project, I designed interfaces of several functions such as Simulation, Deploy, Vision, Error, Scan and Calibration, created high-fidelity interactive mockups and developed interactive specifications. Due to the strong interest in visual programming, I in-depth researched and designed the If-Then Tool.

  • ②
  • I also participated in the research of User-AI collaborative experience, combed the challenges of the experience and designed the journey maps.

  • ③
  • Moreover, I visualized and designed infographics for Siemens’ digital enterprise solutions.
